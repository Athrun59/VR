How to use a camera or a TV capture device

  1. Download and install VLC
  2. Open VLC and go to "Media->Open capture device.."
  3. Set capture mode to Directshow and select your camera or other capture device in the drop down list
  4. Click on the "Show more options" check box
  5. Copy the MRL (Media url) link and paste to VR Player using "File->Open->Stream->With VLC"
